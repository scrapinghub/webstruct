Hewlett-Woodmere School District

The Hewlett-Woodmere school district continues to maintain excellence by scoring exceedingly higher than Nassau County and NYS. Although we are pleased with our students' outstanding achievement, we are cautious about interpreting results of single day tests, which measure a limited portion of our curriculum. Therefore, we continue to value our own district assessments including student performance data that measure students' growth over time and guide the curriculum and instruction we value.
