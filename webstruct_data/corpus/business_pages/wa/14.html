Car Finance Australia

At Fincar, we are specialists. We specialise in - car finance, equipment finance and related insurance matters. We have relationships with a number of respected finance and insurance providers who can assist with these requirements and our sole aim is to find you the best, most competitive finance and insurance solution for your new purchase.

Whether it's a consumer car loan or a short term operating lease, through our extensive panel of lenders we are well placed to be able to find you the most competitive rates, the most suitable repayment strategy and the most effective product with the minimum of fuss and the maximum of professionalism.

All staff have extensive experience in their fields, having strong finance and insurance credentials (FSRA Tier II qualifications) and possessing a customer service ethic that means that our valued clients are treated with the professionalism and diligence that they deserve.

FinCar is based in Chatswood on Sydney's North Shore and is able to provide finance solutions to any resident within Australia.  Please Contact Us for more information.
