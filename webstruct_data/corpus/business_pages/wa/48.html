Industrial Open Frame Monitor and Touch Screen Monitors

Caltron specializes in embedded industrial products like open frame monitors and open frame touch screen monitors for equipment manufacturers. Since 1997, we have been working to establish strategic partnerships with customers to enable that all needs are met for projects that require industrial grade LCD/LED open frame monitors, interactive open frame touch screen monitors, or industrial touchscreen monitors. Caltron continues to provide new technology display products and also keep top product quality as in the past many years. We can work with OEM/ODM and integrators to meet special project needs. We also specialize in assisting customers by providing the necessary products and tools for a prototype project. Caltron will assist and provide information and technical support that is needed for any product that we have in order to satisfy a customer's need for a project application.
