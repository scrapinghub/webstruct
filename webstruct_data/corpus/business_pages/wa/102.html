Complete-Building is Gloucestershire's premier building company

Covering every aspect of your home from groundwork and drainage to your roof and chimney, we are experts in all of the services we cover and work in a polite, friendly and tidy manner.

We have learnt a lot in our years in the building trade and apply this knowledge to every job. From the top quality materials we always use, to our polite and professional staff, to our outstanding guarantees.

We bring a breath of fresh air to the building industry and our aim is simple but often overlooked. Work professionally, politely, and cleanly. Our customers and their homes are important to us and will always be respected.

Unlike most builders, we do not hide behind mobile phone numbers, we provide you with home and office numbers so you can always be sure of contacting us and we also have office premises that you are welcome to visit.

With the government relaxing local planning laws it is now possible to build reasonable sized extensions and conservatories without the need for planning permission and detailed architects' drawings. Building regulations must still be adhered to and all aspects of the project inspected by building control officers to ensure it meets the required standards.

At complete Building we always insist that every project is handled correctly, inspected, and signed off by building control. We will deal with this entire process for you and ensure all works are to regulation. When works are complete you will be issued with your completion certificate. Without this certificate you will not be able to prove the quality of the work and may have problems when you sell your house.
