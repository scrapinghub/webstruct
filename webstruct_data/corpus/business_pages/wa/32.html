Employment

A place where people prosper
After a quarter-century of financing home ownership, we've learned that the right people are important to success. The difference between a good company and a great one can be measured by the quality of the people who work there. At First Cal, we are always looking for the right people to join our growing enterprise. We invite every new associate to help us build for the future.


Our vision and values

We envision a company that ranks in the top ten in loan volume in California, the nation's most active real estate market. A company that places people ahead of loan products, where our associates make a concerted effort to match every customer to the right loan.

A business culture marked by professionalism, personal development and straightforward communication. As we progress we create prosperity, for our customers, our business partners and our associates.
 

If you share our vision and our values, and you want to grow professionally in a business that shapes its customers' lives, send us your story. Tell us about yourself, your experience and career objectives and we'll give you serious consideration.
