About Us

Fundraising Skills

Fundraising Skills is an award winning e-learning organisation offering accredited fundraising courses since 2002. We have helped staff and volunteers from 2000 small charities and schools increase income and improve fundraising management.

Money back guarantee

Our courses are a great investment in your professional development and include accreditation from NCFE. In return for your significant investment, we believe you deserve excellence and you have a right to expect tangible results when you implement what you have learnt. That's why we offer a unique money back guarantee: If within 12 months of completing a course you haven't raised a sum equal to the fee, we'll refund your fee in its entirety. See Terms & conditions for money back guarantee

We also offer a small charity discount and produce a Guide to grants for training, so you can complete quality accredited training that produces results.

Team Members

Louise Faith, Course Director

Louise has 7 years grants assessment experience for major funding bodies. One of the first National Lottery assessors in the UK, Louise was involved in the grant making policy for what became Awards for All. With a background in community work, regeneration and European funding, she brings a strong focus on improving bid writing and funding strategies for small charities. An e-learning specialist, qualified trainer with the Chartered Institute of Personnel Development and past winner of the Entrepreneur of the Year Award.

Anne Daly, Tutor

Anne has a financial and business background and for the past 10 yrs has been applying these skills to the not for profit sector in Scotland. She is an active and experienced fundraiser and champion of small charities. Anne is a Director of her local Community Development Trust and a Trustee of The Phoenix International Charity, a grant making charity, so is well placed to advise on making applications stand out. Anne is an experienced fundraising tutor and has worked with a number of schools on eco strategies.

Kathy Roddy Tutor

Kathy has worked in the voluntary sector for 17 years she was Director of a national animal welfare charity and is now a freelance trainer and consultant specialising in fundraising and strategic development. Kathy has advised a number of charities across most sectors. the arts (BAFTA) social welfare (women.s refuges) environment (Windmill Community Allotment Project) animal welfare (Compassion in World Farming World Society for the Protection of Animals) and many more! Having worked on a number of successful Big Lottery bids and other funding applications Kathy advises on individual and major donor fundraising and legacy fundraising and holds the Certificate in Fundraising Management.

Ellouise James, Course Administrator

Ellouise is a highly experienced administrator managing accreditation procedures and student enrolment at Fundraising Skills. Having completed FE training in Business and Admin, she worked as a Conveyancing Secretary and for the NHS as an Administration Officer and PA. Ellouise is also a Counsellor and as a hobby has her own pet website.
