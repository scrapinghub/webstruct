  • Free Shipping Policy and Details
    • FEDEX Ground shipping is free on orders of $2,500.00 or more.
    • Expedited shipping is available for extra charges (i.e. Next or 2nd Day)

    • SourceTech quotes prices for equipment sales by request. Quotes are also issued for surplus equipment customers wish to sell.

      • Export of these products requires prior written authorization from the US Dept of Commerce.

      • A 15% restocking fee is billed for all returns. Call for an RMA number first. Merchandise returned without a visible RMA will not be accepted by the SourceTech receiving department. Returns must be complete with all original packing materials for refunds.
  • Terms
    • Qualifying Terms are net 30 unless otherwise stated in writing.
    • Title of all equipment shall remain with seller until invoice is paid in full.
