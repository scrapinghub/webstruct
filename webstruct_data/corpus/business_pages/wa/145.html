Inspirational Interior Architecture for the Leisure Industry

Our interiors are inspired by one thing: your business. Before we put pencil to paper we find out everything there is to know about what you do and how you do it. When and only when we have a full picture of your ethos, your customers and your operation, we work with you to give you an interior that enhances your clients’ experience of your brand.

Interiors that bring Brand and Space to life

Specialising in interior architecture for the leisure industry, we design interiors for health and fitness clubs, spas, bars, restaurants, hotels and offices.

Our creativity combined with operational understanding gives us the insight to develop dynamic entertainment spaces that create atmosphere yet are effortlessly functional.

Our hotel and spa interiors are designed to be luxurious havens that are exciting yet also create a sense of relaxation and wellbeing. And our office spaces are stimulating, refreshing workspaces for thriving businesses.

We’ve been working with private individuals, entrepreneurs and large companies and corporations since 1998, and as the case studies on this site show, we’ve created some amazing interiors and we’ve had a lot of fun along the way.
