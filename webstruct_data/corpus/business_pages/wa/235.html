About Aphrodite Cosmetics…

    Born and living in Greece you can hear older people talking about the remedies they prepare to effectively treat health problems or skin sensitivities. Studying the culture and habits of several areas all over Greece, we found that most of these remedies were based on the Olive oil and the wild herbs.

    In Crete, people who live in the small villages, are still following the old traditional Cretan diet and way of living. These people rarely take an aspirin to treat a cold and most of them grow 90-100 years old with no serious health problems.

    Researches have shown that Cretans had the lower percent of heart diseases and they claim that the reason is the healthy Cretan diet, which is based in the use of olive oil.

    However, olive oil was not only richly used in Cretan diet. Greek history refers olive oil to serve beauty too. Centuries ago, in the Minoan period, women were brushing their hair with olive oil to keep them soft and shiny. In ancient Greece, during the Olympic Games, the athletes massaged their bodies with olive oil blended with aromatic plants to calm their muscles and relax. Even nowadays, Greek pediatricians recommend mothers to bathe their newborn babies with olive oil soap in order to avoid allergies or skin inflammations.

Selecting all this information, we realized that indeed Homer was right to refer olive oil as a "golden juice", since it is full of healthy and beauty benefits! That's why we chose the olive oil and wild herbs to be the main ingredients in all the soaps and cosmetics of our selection.

    Aphrodite cosmetics, was the first to discover and appreciate the value of Olive oil in skin, face & hair, and cosmetic care. In Crete, we produce several varieties of Olive oil. That's why it requires the knowledge and experience to know which variety is suitable in each skin care product and create soaps and cosmetics of excellent quality.

    Aphrodite's collection is specially made to satisfy the whole family. From babies to our very senior population.

    All products are produced according to the European regulations and approved from the National Drug Administration to be safe for use.
