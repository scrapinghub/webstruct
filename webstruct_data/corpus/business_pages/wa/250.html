MDP Woodworks & Design

.

MDP Woodworks & Design is a family owned and operated custom furniture and cabinetry shop, specializing in one of a kind, hand crafted, furniture pieces and sets, distinctive cabinetry and architectural installations. Our designs emphasize the natural raw beauty and strength of wood and its complementary materials.

Whether your tastes encompass a rustic Western Style or Fine Cabinets and built-in casework, MDP WOODWORKS & DESIGN can provide the distinctive character that a person is looking for in a one-of-a-kind heirloom quality piece of furniture.

MDP WOODWORKS & DESIGN was begun in 1976 as The Homestead in Stephenville, Texas. At that time we specialized in antique reproductions, repair and refinishing. We also carried unique and one-of-a-kind antiques.Today, we have developed our custom and distinctive offerings, as well as accepting commissions for your individual furnishings.
