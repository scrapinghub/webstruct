Your Fenton Dentist

Proudly serving Fenton, Missouri and beyond …

At DeCo-Dental Family and Cosmetic Dentistry, we take pride in providing top quality dental healthcare.

Our team specializes in family dentistry – giving you peace of mind. The team at DeCo-Dental Family and Cosmetic Dentistry is highly committed to helping you maintain beautiful, healthy smiles.

Your South County dentist is dedicated to serving families of St. Louis Missouri and surrounding areas. Using the latest technologies in dental care our priority is to provide you with family-friendly comprehensive dental care in a welcoming comfortable environment.

The team at DeCo-Dental Family and Cosmetic Dentistry knows that a healthy, attractive smile can change a life. Our team works together to ensure your continued oral health. By focusing on preventative care techniques, our dental hygienists work closely with our patients, helping them to achieve the best dental health.

Our vision as a dentist in Fenton, is to provide total patient satisfaction in all aspects of your visit to our office by providing quality and state-of-the-art dentistry.

No dental insurance? No problem! We’re proud to offer a Deco-Dental Membership Plan. It’s quick and easy to enroll -– find out more. We also offer interest-free payment plans through Care Credit.

A picture perfect smile is always attainable at DeCo-Dental Family and Cosmetic Dentistry, located in the St. Louis area. We specialize in cosmetic and family dentistry, where the end result is patients feeling proud and confident to smile their picture perfect smile -- and feeling good about referring their family and friends to us.

To learn more about DeCo-Dental Family and Cosmetic Dentistry and our services, contact your Fenton dentist today.
