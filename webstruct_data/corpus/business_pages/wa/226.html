Unforgettable Smile Welcomes You!
 

Unforgettable Smile is right at the heart of unforgettable Hollywood makeovers in Beverly Hills, providing amazing and natural smiles. Our dental practice is located just minutes away from the famous Rodeo Drive and the Sunset Strip.

Once you arrive, you'll be treated to a wide range of refreshments, music and CDs available for relaxation. Our dedicated Beverly Hills cosmetic dentistry team in Beverly Hills even goes above and beyond. If you are a sedation dentistry patient, we'll even drive you to and from your appointment if needed.

At Unforgettable Smile, we have chosen only the latest and greatest dental tools and technologies. Our mission is to bring everything you need for health and beauty, even the restoration lab, together in one practice for your ultimate convenience.

In addition to offering amazing technology, we've assembled a team of truly passionate experts who want you to have the beautiful, long-lasting smile you deserve. Each team member works hard to make sure you feel comfortable and carefree. At any point, feel free to talk to us about your comfort level.

Each Beverly Hills dentist in our office has had world-class training, so they can provide you with the most amazing results. Their training includes the prestigious Misch Implant Institute, Cosmetic and Neuromuscular Dentistry courses from LVI, International Congress of Oral Implantology and the AACD.
