Rethink your closets
with glass custom
closet doors.
Most of us never think of our closet doors as a decorating tool, but The Sliding Door Company can help to change the way you think. Imagine custom mirrored or frosted glass closet sliding doors that can change the entire look of a room and provide you with easy access to the contents behind those modern doors. Our modern closet doors are not only stylish, they are safe and glide easily, as well. With a variety of frames and sizes, as well as customizable closet slide doors available, The Sliding Door Company has the closet doors that you need to create the look you desire. Plus, you can choose from an extensive array of glass types including clear, frosted, and black.

No more pockets: beautiful sliding wall doors create a system too beautiful to hide.
 
Many homes have utilized the space-saving benefits of pocket doors. While such a technique does eliminate the necessary clearance required for swing doors, it was previously thought that sliding pocket doors are not much to look at. Thankfully, The Sliding Door Company can change all of that with wall slide doors that are both practical and beautiful. Reap the advantages in terms of the space of pocket doors but gain the beauty of a sliding interior glass door that will move effortlessly along the outside of the wall instead of inside the wall. Beautiful frame finishes and glass types will accent any room of your home and you will love the barely audible, gentle swoosh of the exclusive roller system that will allow for the easiest and quietest movement of your slide door imaginable.
 

Create a perfect space with glass wall dividers, custom interior glass sliding doors, and room divider screens.
Whether your perfect space includes expansive, wide open rooms with clear lines of sight and an abundance of natural light or you enjoy more intimate spaces with a cozy, intimate closeness suitable for reading, studying, or important conversations, The Sliding Door Company has indoor sliding doors, glass room dividers, and sliding room partitions that offer a range of opportunities for you to create the space that you have envisioned. Our modern sliding wardrobe closet doors, sliding door dividers, and room separators enable you to create cozy nooks, intimate spaces, or achieve a panoramic, spacious feel all with the gorgeous, contemporary look of stunning glass and frame types. With The Sliding Door Company, you can have the space of your dreams at work or at home.

The Sliding Door Company is perfect for your office space.

 Sliding glass doors, walls, and partitions can be of incredible benefit in your office space. Tired of having your employees laboring under the institutional glow of fluorescent lights while sitting in uninspiring cubicles or in cramped office spaces? Imagine what it would mean for you and your employees to be bathed in a constant flow of natural light where they have the freedom to work privately at one moment and then collaboratively the next thanks to exciting new floor plans made possible by The Sliding Door Company. Not only can office cubicles, partitions, and privacy walls be given new life with the installation of glass panels, interior doors, and sliding walls, but you may very well enjoy an increase in productivity, creativity, and office morale, as well.

Enjoy the natural beauty of wall sliding doors, shoji sliding doors, office room dividers, and decorative room dividers.

 

If you are concerned that a closet sliding door, frosted glass French doors, or a loft room divider will not fit with your current dcor and would seem out of place, rest assured that a sliding glass door, partition, or wall door from The Sliding Door Company can help to tie rooms together with their sleek elegance and beauty.

The sparkling brightness and timelessness of glass, combined with frames in a variety of finishes and grains, can be custom designed to match any look that you want to achieve while still providing you with the distinctive functionality and sleek glamour of contemporary glass room dividers, Shoji screens, interior French doors, and more.
