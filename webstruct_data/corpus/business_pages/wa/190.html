Executive Vision s Inc. (EVI) is a leading producer of corporate communications and brand-immersive experiences. This Primetime Emmy winning company offers a full range of services including the creative development design production and staging of corporate meetings celebrity entertainment concerts & events product launches training & education programs marketing communications consulting and related multimedia. These services are delivered in all forms of media including live events broadcast television film & video print exhibits displays & installations websites DVD and other interactive technologies. Executive Vision s Inc was founded in 1986.

EVI designs solutions organically which enable corporations to better inform, entertain, motivate, recognize, sell to and train their target audiences including: sales forces, dealers, franchisees, partners, customers, and employees.

EVIs Clients are typically large companies that have a business need to communicate with key internal and external audiences and include some of the world's largest companies across many industries. Major clients include BellSouth, Coca-Cola, Harley-Davidson, Holiday Inn Worldwide, IBM, McDonald's, Parke-Davis, and Rolls-Royce/Bentley Motor Cars.

EVI has established a track record of success in executing high-profile Projects of various types and sizes, including multi-million dollar events and training programs. These projects often involve precision integration and expert project management of diverse creative, production, and technical talents to maximize results within the time-frame and budget of the client. EVI's principal strengths are its ability to:

     1) Effectively assemble and manage numerous in-house and out-of-house resources,
     2) Consistently meet client objectives and expectations with the optimal solution, and
     3) Reliably manage a number of complex large-scale projects contemporaneously.

Founded on the principles of excellence, price performance, and integrity, EVI is committed to achieving success for its clients by applying the full power of communications as an extension of business strategy and execution.
