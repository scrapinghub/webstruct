Multiple Locations to Best Serve Your Dental Needs.

Imagine going to the dentist and feeling comfortable while getting the beautiful teeth you have always wanted, in fewer visits. From orthodontic treatment to the restoration of missing teeth, SC Dental Group staff provide you with the finest dental care to protect and maintain your oral health.

Our doctors offer specialized services to enhance your comfort and health with greater convenience for your busy schedule:

Hate the sound of dental drills? Waterlase® utilizes water and laser technology to replace the friction and buzzing of the drill. In many cases, no anesthetic is needed, meaning no needles and no need to wait for numbing to take effect.

Are you unable to schedule time off from work to accommodate multiple dental visits? Dr. Chang utilizes CEREC® to design and create your fillings, crowns, and veneers in just one visit to our same day dentist. Final, permanent restorations are placed in the same visit; no temporaries are worn. Even if you need work performed on multiple teeth, you can schedule just one appointment.

Do you experience dental anxiety? You can rest easy during your appointment with sedation dental treatments. If you avoided having dental work performed due to fear of dentistry, you can finally relax and schedule your visit, with sedation dentistry.

Have you lost your teeth to injury, decay or gum disease? We offer treatments that can replace one tooth or all of your teeth with permanent, natural looking dental implants. A versatile solution, dental implants can improve your confidence while preserving your health.


Are you pressed for time?   CEREC same day crowns
Our highly trained dentists utilize CEREC technology to fabricate same day crowns.  No need for temporaries, impressions, and a second visit to seat your crown.

Do you experience dental anxiety?  Sedation Dentistry is for you
We offer Sedation Dentistry, allowing to relax completely for your dental treatment.  Also, for patients needing extensive dental treatment, Sedation Dentistry allows our highly trained team to complete a substantial amount of treatment in one day.


Does the cost of Dentistry keep you from Smiling Confidently?
We offer a variety of affordable payment options, including Care Credit (click here for Care Credit approval application), we take most insurance plans, and offer a program for patients without Dental Insurance.



Schedule New Dental Patient Appointments Today

 SC Dental Group is accepting new dental patient appointments. If you are looking for a new Scottsdale, AZ family dentist, contact our office to schedule a visit. You will find that we place a priority on your health and comfort. We look forward to meeting you!

Tired Of Hiding Your Gum Recession?

Dr. Steven Chang offers the advanced Pinhole Surgical Technique for gum grafting that has revolutionized the treatment of gum recession. Dr. Chang was trained by Dr. John Chao DDS JD the inventor and patent-owner of Pinhole Surgical Technique with personal guidance. In fact Dr. Chang regularly communicates with Dr. John Chao for clinical findings and advanced training.

If you have gum recession seek treatment from the President of Pinhole Surgical Technique Study Group for Certified Clinicians in Arizona Dr. Steven Chang! We offer flexible weekend and late hours for patients who want to improve the health and appearance of their smile with PST. Most dental insurance covers the procedure and financing is available for qualified patients including iCare and Care Credit. Call us today to get started!
