The Perk APP Family:
Perk Shopping
Earn points when you shop at over 3,000 partner stores.

Shop with Perk and earn points you can exchange for gift cards and other great rewards. Shop at over 3,000 stores in one easy-to-use app. With Perk, you can easily shop and find coupons from top brands including Target, Macy's, Best Buy, Groupon, Nordstrom, and more.

Available on iOS

Perk Search
Instantly earn points when you search the web.

Get points when you search the web. It's easy. Perk Search is also integrated with IMDB, Wikipedia, YouTube, Yelp, and Twitter.

Available on iOS and Android.

Perk Screen
Unlock your phone and win points and prizes!

Get points and prizes for unlocking your phone! Discover new apps, brands, and deals delivered right to your screen! Simply swipe left or right for a chance to win. Every time you unlock your phone, you could win!

Available on Android.

Perk Browser
Get rewarded for doing what you already do online.

Discover a more rewarding web with the Perk Browser. Earn points when you search and shop. Check-in to your favorite places online and get coupons and deals at your favorite stores. Other features include one-tap privacy, an integrated link shortener, and social sharing.

Available on iOS and Android.

Perk TV
Earn points for watching entertaining videos!

Watch videos and earn points on the go with Perk TV! Check out the latest movie, app, and game trailers, along with other entertaining videos on your phone or tablet! With Perk TV, you can earn points anywhere and instantly when you watch.

Available on iOS and Android.
