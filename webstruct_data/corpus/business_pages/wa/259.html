A few words from our clients

  • Rajani Liubov
    2014-01-28

    I just wanted to let you know that Customer service coordinator Kasia Kerfont was very helpful , polite and appreciative of me being their customer.My Bedroom furniture had some defects , but she dealt with this issue in a very prompt and professional manner. Thank you Kasia! Also would like to mention Alla and Renata, salespersons. They are very helpful, patient and polite too! Surely will come back for more furniture to your store! Thanks again!

  • Nayla Badine
    2014-01-26

    I had a very satisfactory experience with Decorium's Customer Service Coordinator Kasia Kerfont. I had brought to her attention a defect issue I was having with a leather accent chair I had purchased almost one year prior. Kasia very promptly escalated my concern with the manufacturer of the chair and within 10 days called me back to let me know that the manufacturer had indeed experienced quality issues with this product in the past. She offered me a re-selection stating that I could choose another accent chair on my next visit to the store. If it was of lesser value I would get a store credit. I was very happy with her advocacy on this matter and the quick turnaround time overall. She was also very kind respectful empathetic and appreciative of my business. This experienced showed me that Decorium stands by their customers and cares about their experience. THANK YOU AGAIN Kasia! -- Nayla

  • Tracy Wechsler
    2014-01-24

    I just wanted to let you know how thrilled we are with all the furniture we have purchased for our living and family rooms. All the staff especially Bill and Alla have a wonderful demeanor and were very helpful. They took their time to help me find the exact pieces for each room and when I had a special request to customize a sofa you came through. The sofa is perfect. I am thrilled with all the pieces we have purchased. Even the people that delivered the furniture were great. Decorium is an incredible place to shop for quality home furniture.

  • Sarah davies
    2013-12-05

    I just wanted to tell you what a great experience I had picking up my table. Chris in receiving was amazing as was the rest of the team there. They met me with smiles and were so polite and thoughtful. The entire team made the pick up easy and I will without a doubt be a return customer. This was my first purchase from you and I look forward to many more in the years to come. Happy Holidays and a Happy New Year, Sarah Davies
