Who We Are

MDP Woodworks & Design is a family owned and operated custom furniture and cabinetry shop, specializing in one of a kind, hand crafted, furniture pieces and sets, distinctive cabinetry and architectural installations. Our designs emphasize the natural raw beauty and strength of wood and its complimentary materials.

Whether your tastes encompass the rustic Western Style or Fine Cabinets and built-in casework, MDP WOODWORKS & DESIGN can provide the distinctive character that a person is looking for in a one-of-a-kind heirloom quality piece of furniture.

MDP WOODWORKS & DESIGN was begun in 1976 as The Homestead in Stephenville, Texas. At that time we specialized in antique reproductions, repair and refinishing. We also carried unique and one-of-a-kind antiques.Today, we have developed our custom and distinctive offerings, as well as accepting commissions for your individual furnishings.

Furniture designer and builder Michael Pate began his wood working experiences with his grandfather as a child. He has worked with top end furniture stores as well as extensively studying Early American Furniture and their craftsmen. The furniture of the settlers of Texas has been his focus for since 1976 with a true appreciation of the artisans who constructed it. He has also taught cabinet making and enjoys sharing the true beauty that wood has to offer.

Along with his wife Diana, they have a unique working partnership that allows ideas to flow and develop into the unique offerings of MDP Woodworks & Design. Diana's eye for color and interior design, and her love of nature and landscaping, brings a natural and fitting element to the pieces produced.
