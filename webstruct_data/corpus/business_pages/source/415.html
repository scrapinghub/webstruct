About A&Js' Bike Locks

A&Js' Bike Locks, a division of Orland Park Cyclery, is located in Orland Park, Illinois. We are a family owned and operated, full-service bicycle shop. We can assist you with nearly any cycling-related issue you may have, including:

  • Building and maintaining quality bicycles
  • Finding great deals on reliable gear
  • Getting great bicycle advice for the amateur or experienced cyclist

We know what were talking about because were cyclists ourselves. When were not helping clients, youll probably find us riding, racing, or touring the country on our own bikes. Thats why our clients trust us to provide the best information, bicycles, bike locks, and other equipment.
