A Southern Oregon Hotel Surrounded By Beauty

Situated among the natural wonders and mesmerizing beauty of southwest Oregon, Holiday Inn Express & Suites Canyonville is a welcoming retreat for a vacation in the great outdoors with all the comforts of home. Located in Canyonville in Southern Oregon, our hotel is surrounded by the region's forests, parks, waterfalls, lakes, and rivers where you'll discover endless hiking, biking, fishing, and boating adventures. Explore Oregon's third-oldest town, and discover the rich pioneering history of Canyonville, or get into the action at Seven Feathers Casino Resort, a minute drive from the hotel.
 

Oregon Adventures . . . Go Play -

Get wild in the Umpqua National Forest, with a million acres of pristine wilderness for adventure. Enjoy hiking to the stunning Watson Falls and breathe in breathtaking views. If you crave water sports, Southern Oregon is a scenic playground for kayaking, whitewater rafting, fishing, and boating. Our hotel is a short drive from the Umpqua River, a perfect spot for bass, trout, salmon and steelhead fishing. Whether you prefer a serene paddle or adventurous rapids, the scenic Umpqua River - flowing through the Oregon Coast Range to the Pacific Ocean - is kayaking and whitewater rafting heaven.
