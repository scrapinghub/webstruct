Reservations - The Heathman Hotel

Enjoy the perfect vacation or business trip to Kirkland, Washington when you stay at The Heathman Hotel in the heart of town. Often referred to as the Sausalito of Seattle, Kirkland is located minutes from Seattle and Bellevue. The Heathman Hotel is one of the top luxury hotels in the Greater Seattle area, offering an authentic mix of friendly Northwest hospitality and contemporary chic personality.

Contact our attentive hotel reservations team and start planning your next Seattle /Kirkland getaway. Ask about our new hotel packages, high-tech meeting and event space, and offers for two complimentary tickets aboard Argosy Cruises from April - September! Make your hotel reservation online for the best rates on our Washington hotel rooms. Not only will you find the best rates online, but we do not charge hotel reservation booking fees. While staying at The Heathman Hotel, enjoy complimentary luxury, Hybrid SUV transportation to local shopping and business districts on the Eastside. Ssimply contact Guest Services. Transportation via our SUV to pick-up or drop-off at any of the area airports may also be arranged (for fee).

We look forward to welcoming guests to The Heathman Hotel in Kirkland, Washington - Where Service is Still an Art
