The Arizona Riverpark Inn is the perfect place to stay in Tucson.  Our comfortable AAA 3-Diamond full-service hotel just a short walk from the Tucson Convention Center and all of downtown Tucson's highlights.  Tucson is known for its Arts and unique historic sites, galleries, museums, and performance spaces Tucson offers spectacular landscapes, fascinating landmarks, and rich heritage. Numerous restaurants, shops, golf courses and other local attractions are also nearby.  We are also a short drive from the campuses of the University of Arizona and Pima Community College.

Tucson Attractions.

   It would be hard to find a more conveniently-located hotel than the Arizona Riverpark Inn Tucson.  Tucson's AMTRAK Station is less than two miles away and Tucson International Airport is within seven miles.  There is plenty of shopping within five miles, including the largest outdoor shopping center in southern Arizona, the new Tucson Spectrum which major retailers such as Best Buy, J.C. Penney, Bed Bath & Beyond, Shoe Pavilion, Lane Bryant and Kirkland's home decor.  In addition there is a multiplex movie theatre, Harkins Theatres, with 18 movie screens.  Other local attractions such as Old Tucson Studios, Saguaro National Park, San Xavier Mission, Sabino Canyon, the Arizona Sonora Desert Museum and Tucson's four casinos are just a comfortable drive away.  With a strong downtown arts scene, there are numerous theatrical productions, ballets, operas and music presentations.

Driving Directions I-10 Construction is Complete The tunnel to the Convention Center is now open!

I-10 Freeway construction was completed September 2009.  Direct access to the Tucson Convention Center (225 FT wide Cushing Street underpass opened September 2009.)              

The following Tucson directions are detailed below

  • Directions from the North and West (Phoenix, Los Angeles & San Diego)
  • Directions from the East (New Mexico and El Paso)
  • Directions from the South (Green Valley, Nogales & Sonora)
  • Directions from Tucson Airport

Directions from the North & West (Phoenix, Los Angeles and San Diego)

  • Get off I-10 at the Congress Street Exit # 258
  • Continue straight ahead on the southbound Frontage Road
  • The Arizona Riverpark Inn will be on the right -- ½ block past Congress Street.  

Directions from the East (New Mexico and El Paso)

  • Get off I-10 at the 29th Street Exit # 258
  • Turn left at Congress Street and go underneath the Freeway
  • Take an immediate left turn at Carl’s Jr. onto the southbound Frontage Road
  • The Arizona Riverpark Inn will be on the right ½ block ahead.

Directions from the South (Green Valley, Nogales and Sonora)

  • Get off I-19 North and merge onto I-10 West
  • Get off I-10 West at the Congress Street Exit # 258 
  • Turn left at Congress Street and go underneath the Freeway
  • Take an immediate left turn at Carl’s Jr. onto the southbound Frontage Road
  • The Arizona Riverpark Inn will be on the right ½ block ahead.

Directions from Tucson International Airport

  • Exit Tucson International Airport on Tucson Boulevard and continue straight
  • Turn left onto Valencia Road
  • Turn right onto I-19 North
  • Get off I-19 North and merge onto I-10 West
  • Get off I-10 West at the Congress Street Exit # 258
  • Turn left at Congress Street and go underneath the Freeway
  • Take an immediate left turn at Carl’s Jr. onto the southbound Frontage Road
  • The Arizona Riverpark Inn will be on the right ½ block ahead.
