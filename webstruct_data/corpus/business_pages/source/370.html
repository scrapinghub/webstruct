Find Us

Our showroom in Ilford, Essex, just outside London, is easily accessible from all major roads and motorways and offers a comprehensive range of our wholesale cosmetics, perfumes, accessories, and costume and fashion jewellery. 

We are situated on the main A12 near Gants Hill roundabout, our address and opening hours are;

 

Chas & Phil Wholesale,
295 Eastern Avenue,
Gants Hill, Ilford,
ESSEX IG2 6NT

 

 

Opening Times:

Monday to Friday 09:30 till 17:30

 

Thursday 09:30 till 18:30

 

Saturday Closed

 

Sunday 10:00 till 14:00

By road

From M25 or M11.
From M25 take the M11 towards London. Take the A12 Gants Hill turn-off which leads to a large roundabout. Take 1st main exit signposted GANTS HILL A12. Continue until first major set of traffic lights. We are situated 15 yards further, on the left hand side.

From A13
Continue until Beckon roundabout - at roundabout follow signs for A406 NORTH CIRCULAR. Continue until signs for A12. Take the A12 exit which leads to a large roundabout - at roundabout take 3rd main exit signposted A12 GANTS HILL. Continue until first major set of traffic lights. We are situated 15 yards further on the left hand side.

From A406
Continue until you reach Gants Hill roundabout - at roundabout take 4th exit signposted A12 LONDON - continue until first set of traffic lights. Take filter lane on right hand side at traffic lights. Look to your right and you will be looking straight at us.

You may park on our forecourt at the front of the wholesale jewellery warehouse, and remember you can order our entire range of fashion jewellery, hair accessories, bags, cosmetics and perfumes for delivery throughout the UK including London, Birmingham, Leeds, Glasgow, Sheffield, Bradford, Edinburgh, Liverpool, Manchester, Bristol, Wakefield, Cardiff, Coventry, Nottingham, Leicester, Sunderland, Newcastle, Brighton, Hull, Plymouth.
