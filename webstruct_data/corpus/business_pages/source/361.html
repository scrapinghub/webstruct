We specialise in A4 sheets of blank labels suitable for use with laser and inkjet printers. Our customers can choose from over 100 sizes across a range of 30 different materials, speciality adhesives, and colours. Along with a fantastic selection of labels, our website features free templates, a sample request service, and a variety of helpful guides to designing and printing labels.

All of the prices on our website include VAT and standard UK delivery, and we provide superb same day despatch on orders of 25, 50, or 100 sheets of stocked items. We offer our customers the most extensive range of stocked label products on A4 sheets in the UK. Orders of 500+ sheets of matt white paper labels are generally despatched on the next working day, while all other orders of 500+ sheets are despatched within five working days.

Label Planet is a family owned company that has been selling labels online for over 10 years. Currently working from a leafy business park in Nantwich, Cheshire, we support our customers with years of experience in the world of labels, from choosing the right label for the job to help and advice on getting the perfect print. We supply quality products at excellent value and offer genuine customer service from making an order through to getting the finished product just right.
