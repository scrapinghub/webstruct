General Principles of EDTP

 

General principles of the EDTP implementation support to Azerbaijani firms:

  • Development Advisory services
  • Working closely with Shareholders, senior management, and employees to ensure that the development approaches are suitable for the company needs.
  • Endeavoring to ensure that both management and the staff understand the business implications of the development efforts.

Supporting in effective implementation of development program.
Providing advanced local and international expertise
