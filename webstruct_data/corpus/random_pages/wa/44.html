What's Happening at AutoGen

AutoGen and NorDiag Sign Distribution Agreement for North America…

AutoGen announces entering into a distribution agreement with Nordiag Inc. US subsidiary for distribution of NorDiag products to the Life Science market in North America.


NorDiag is a leading developer and manufacturer of sample preparation instrumentation and technologies for use in both life science research as well as diagnostic applications. NorDiag’s Arrow instrument is the only system of its kind that can perform both nucleic acid extraction and cell separation. Autogen, leaders in the nucleic acid extraction market, have a significant client portfolio of research institutions, laboratories and hospitals to which they market their nucleic acid extraction systems.


The agreement includes distribution of instruments and chemistry specifically designed for the life sciences market. The agreement has a time frame of 5 years with an option for a further 2 years. The parties have not released financial terms of the agreement, but NorDiag expects that AutoGen will be able to make a significant sales impact in 2012 and beyond.


About NorDiag:


NorDiag is a biotechnology company developing, manufacturing and marketing automated solutions (instruments and reagents) for sample preparation of DNA from difficult biological samples. DNA diagnostics give more rapid and precise answers, and are the fastest growing field within diagnostics. The Company's sample preparation solutions are today used in connection with STI, tuberculosis, MRSA, respiratory pathogens and viruses on instruments for large and small laboratories. NorDiag was founded in 2003 and has its headquarters in Oslo, Norway. The Company has offices and laboratories in Stockholm, Sweden and in Atlanta (GA), USA.


AutoGen to distribute Qiagen’s FlexiGene chemistry…

AutoGen and Qiagen have recently signed an agreement that appoints AutoGen to be an authorized distributor of Qiagen’s FlexiGene whole blood DNA extraction chemistry. AutoGen will sell the automated version of the kit for use with the FLEX STAR, their automated workstation for large volume whole blood DNA extraction. In addition AutoGen will sell the manual version of the FlexiGene kit for low throughput manual applications.


AutoGen will soon be able to offer the automated version of the FlexiGene kit to its installed base of FLEX STAR users. For more details call AutoGen at 508-429-5965 x 37, or email us at info@autogen.com .
